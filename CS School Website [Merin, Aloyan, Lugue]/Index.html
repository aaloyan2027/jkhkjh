About Us

Founded in 1963, the Philippine Science Academy provides a higher quality education for those passionate about STEM and excelling in STEM. Our education system will nurture our students to grow spiritually and academically, preparing them for the perils to come in college. Furthermore, They shall uphold the qualities of honesty, respect, and cooperation in their pursuit of excellence and later in their servitude to the people and the country.
