SECTION 3: SIMULATION OF BiLE ACTION


  By using water, oil, and dishwashing liquid, we simulated the function of bile in digestion. Mixing the dishwashing liquid with the water and oil mixture represents the bile being mixed with the fats present in the food. In our digestive system, bile breaks down fats into fatty acids during chemical digestion in the duodenum and emulsifies it to become a smooth mixture. In the experiment, the oil and water had distinct layers but once the dish soap was added and mixed, all the liquids were combined into one mixture.
