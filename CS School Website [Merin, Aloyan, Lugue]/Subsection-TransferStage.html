SUBSECTION: TRANSFER STAGE


Transfer Stage Experiment


 After the crushing stage, we poured the contents into a stocking, simulating how nutrients were being absorbed in the small intestine and carried out to the other parts of the body when we squeezed the liquid into the tray. With the help of a cup functioning as a funnel, we pushed the food down the stocking mimicking peristalsis, and squeezed the liquid from the mixture. What’s left in the stocking is waste that didn’t get absorbed in the small and large intestines, these feces are then eliminated from the body through the rectum and anus.
