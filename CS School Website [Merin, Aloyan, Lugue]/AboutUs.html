HISTORY


Philippine Science Academy was founded in 1963, with hopes of nourishing students with the best education related to STEM. With the goal of fixing the problems with the curriculums being taught at the time, so with the help of various different associations, the academy was founded.

Initially, only the course of Biology, Chemistry, and Physics were taught, but over time, more courses began to be taught, such as Botany, Earth Science, Computer Science, Social Science, and more recently, English and Filipino Literature. The aforementioned courses were added to the curriculum as the academy to expand.

The academy is noteworthy for the achievements it had racked up, including the MIT THINK Scholars program, Regeneron Science Talent Search (STS), and Envirothon.
